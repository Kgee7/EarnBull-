# **App Name**: EarnBull: Walk & Earn

## Core Features:

- Step Tracking & BC Reward: Automatically track user steps and calculate Bull Coin (BC) rewards based on 10 BC per 1,000 steps.
- Bull Coin Wallet: Display the user's BC balance and transaction history, showing earnings and conversions.
- BC to USD Conversion: Enable users to convert their BC to USD at a fixed rate (10 BC = $0.15 USD).
- USD to GHS Exchange: Convert USD earnings to Ghana Cedis (GHS) using the platform's exchange rate. Offer to fetch an actual conversion tool with reasonable numbers.
- MTN Mobile Money Withdrawal: Allow users to withdraw their GHS balance to their MTN Mobile Money account. Enforce a minimum withdrawal threshold of $1 USD.
- Google Authentication: Secure the backend with Google Authentication, ensuring only authenticated users can access their accounts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23), mirroring natural tones from the app's imagery, providing a natural association with both currency and outdoors.
- Background color: Very light green (#F0FFF0), a muted tint from the earthy primary.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700), drawn from the monetary element in the bull image and used to highlight rewards and conversion rates, increasing contrast and recognition.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headings, paired with 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use simple, clean icons to represent steps, Bull Coins, currency exchange, and withdrawals.
- A clean and intuitive layout that prominently displays step count, BC balance, conversion rates, and withdrawal options. Focus on easy navigation and clear information hierarchy.
- Subtle animations to indicate successful step tracking and reward accumulation. Smooth transitions during currency conversions and withdrawal processes to enhance user engagement.
